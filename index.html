Environmental Conservation

Ways you can be the change.

What is Enivronmental Conservation and why is it important?

Environmental conservation involves actively participating in the preservation of Earth's natural environment and resources. By taking proactive steps to safeguard these natural systems, we can contribute to the gradual mitigation of climate change

Climate change refers to significant and long-term shifts in Earth's weather patterns and temperatures, mainly caused by human activities like burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes. This leads to global warming, rising sea levels, extreme weather events, and other environmental disruptions.

What can we do?

Here are some steps you can take to help:

  • Reuse, Reduce, Recycle
    • The things we buy, like electronics, clothes, and plastics, create pollution at every step of their production, from getting the raw materials to making and shipping them. To help the environment, try to buy less stuff, consider buying used items, and fix things when they break instead of replacing them. Plastics, for example, create a lot of pollution. In 2019, they caused 1.8 billion tons of greenhouse gas emissions, which is bad for the climate. Most plastic isn't recycled, so it sticks around for a long time after we throw it away. Buying fewer new clothes and other stuff can also help cut down on pollution. For every kilogram of clothes made, about 17 kilograms of carbon dioxide are released into the air.
  • Clean Up
    • When we don't throw away our garbage properly, it can harm people, animals, and plants by polluting the land and water. Use only what you need, and when you're done with something, make sure to throw it away in the right place. Encourage others to do the same, and join efforts to clean up parks, rivers, beaches, and other areas. Every year, humans throw away a huge amount of trash - about 2 billion tons. A third of this trash causes environmental problems like polluting water and poisoning soil.
  • Speak Up
    • To help protect our planet, let's speak up about the changes we want to see. Encourage everyone in our community and household to make cleaner and more environmentally friendly choices. We can start by reducing waste, conserving energy, using reusable items, and supporting eco-friendly practices. Together, we can make a positive impact on the health of our planet for future generations.

Organizations

World Wide Fund for Nature

The World Wide Fund for Nature is one of the worlds largest earth conservation organizations. They work with communities around the world to help conserve natural resources to protect and restore habitats. The WWF focuses its efforts on six ambitious goals to address urgent global challenges and ensure a healthy future for both people and nature. These goals include creating a climate-resilient and zero-carbon world, rebuilding food systems sustainably, conserving vital forests and freshwater resources, promoting healthy oceans and marine habitats, and protecting wildlife and wild places. Through initiatives such as reducing greenhouse gas emissions, promoting sustainable agriculture, combating deforestation and overfishing, and conserving endangered species, WWF works towards a more sustainable and resilient planet.

GreenPeace

Greenpeace is a global environmental organization dedicated to advocating for a green and peaceful future. Its mission encompasses confronting environmental challenges such as climate change, biodiversity loss, and ocean conservation. Greenpeace works to promote renewable energy, protect ecosystems, and combat overfishing and pollution. Additionally, the organization advocates for peace and disarmament while empowering individuals and communities to take action on environmental issues. Through its campaigns and activism, Greenpeace strives to create a more sustainable and equitable world for both people and the planet.

The Nature Conservacy

The Nature Conservancy (TNC) is a global organization committed to conserving the lands and waters vital for all life. Its mission involves protecting critical habitats like forests and coral reefs, addressing climate change through nature-based solutions, ensuring clean water sources, promoting sustainable agriculture, fostering healthy urban environments, and empowering communities in conservation efforts. TNC's approach emphasizes science-based strategies and collaborative partnerships to safeguard nature for present and future generations, recognizing the interconnectedness between a healthy environment and human well-being.
